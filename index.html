Why Clean Your Dryer Vent?

  • Reduce the risk of house fires caused by lint buildup dryers cause nearly 3,000 residential fires annually in the U.S.
  • Improve dryer efficiency and lower your energy bills a clogged vent can increase drying time and energy use by up to 30%.
  • Extend the life of your appliance overheating and overuse wear out components faster.
  • Clothes dry faster and more evenly stop running your dryer twice just to get dry clothes.
  • Prevent mold and mildew trapped moisture in the vent system can promote fungal growth.
  • Enhance indoor air quality reduce the release of dust and allergens back into your home.
